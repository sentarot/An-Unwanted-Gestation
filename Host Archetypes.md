# **TECHNICAL GDD: Host Archetypes & AI Profiles**

## **1\. Overview of Host Mechanics**

Each Host functions as an autonomous agent with a unique set of baseline stats and a prioritized daily schedule. The player's goal is to strategically select upgrades that exploit the specific weaknesses of the chosen Host, regardless of the gestation type selected.

### **1.1 Core Attribute Definitions**

* **Physical Resistance (Phys):** Resistance to Discomfort. High Phys hosts waddle longer and resist physical debuffs more effectively before suffering stamina penalties.  
* **Mental Defense (Ment):** Resistance to Humiliation and Mind Control. High Ment hosts are harder to emotionally destabilize or psychologically hijack.  
* **Financial Resources (Fin):** The host's ability to pay for "Cures" or "Interventions." High Fin hosts can fill the Intervention\_Meter rapidly through professional medical assistance.  
* **Social Standing (Soc):** Determines how much Biomass is generated from public Humiliation. High Soc hosts provide higher resource yields but have more social tools to fight back.

## **2\. Archetype: The University Elite (The College Cheerleader)**

**Narrative Backdrop:** A top-tier scholarship athlete and sorority president, she is the undisputed queen of the university campus. Her entire identity is built on her athletic agility and her "flawless" petite physique. Her punishment involves a public, biological dismantling of her social rank—forced to waddle through crowded lecture halls and campus plazas as her body reaches impossible, grunting proportions.

* **Baseline Stats:**  
  * **Phys:** 70 (Elite athlete stamina, but high sensitivity to physical "imperfection")  
  * **Ment:** 30 (Fragile ego; desperate for social validation)  
  * **Fin:** 45 (Allowance and athletic stipends; limited independent intervention)  
  * **Soc:** 95 (Campus celebrity; her every waddle is a public event)  
* **Behavioral Pattern:**  
  * **Schedule:** Team Training (Phys), Lecture Hall (Soc), Sorority Mixer (Soc), Campus Plaza (Soc).  
  * **Reaction:** If Humiliation \> 40, she attempts to use "Oversized University Gear" to hide her state. Once Gestation \> 60%, her social circle begins to shift their perception of her, treating her with a mix of pity and fascination that generates increased Biomass.  
* **Unique Vulnerability: Social Fragility.** All Humiliation gains are multiplied by 1.6x. Her reliance on her "perfect" reputation makes her exceptionally easy to break through public spectacle.

**Narrative Backdrop:** A woman of extreme vanity who used her beauty to secure a high-status marriage while maintaining multiple clandestine affairs. * **Baseline Stats:**  
  * **Phys:** 20 (Soft, pampered, and physically weak)  
  * **Ment:** 50 (Stubborn and used to getting her way)  
  * **Fin:** 100 (Access to elite medical accounts)  
  * **Soc:** 85 (High-society circles)  
* **Behavioral Pattern:**  
  * **Schedule:** Spa/Wellness (Phys), Charity Gala (Soc), High-End Dining (Fin), Private Clinic (Intervention).  
  * **Reaction:** She will prioritize Seek\_Doctor and Intervention tasks above all else once she detects the condition. Her high Fin allows her to reach the 80% threshold on the Intervention\_Meter very quickly if her funds aren't drained.  
* **Unique Vulnerability: Pampered Frame.** All Discomfort and physical strain effects are 2x more effective. Her body has zero tolerance for the weight and gravity of a heavy gestation.

## **4\. Archetype: The Ruthless Executive (The "Girlboss")**

**Narrative Backdrop:** A cold, hyper-competent CEO who views any form of physical vulnerability or "domesticity" as a professional failure. She has spent her career breaking glass ceilings and ruthlessly firing underperformers. Her punishment is the ultimate loss of control: her sharp, analytical mind being clouded by hormones while her body becomes a slow, heavy, and undeniably "unprofessional" spectacle in the boardroom.

* **Baseline Stats:**  
  * **Phys:** 50 (Workaholic stamina; neglects her body)  
  * **Ment:** 90 (Iron-willed and logically grounded)  
  * **Fin:** 85 (Personal wealth and corporate insurance)  
  * **Soc:** 75 (Respected but feared; status is tied to her competence)  
* **Behavioral Pattern:**  
  * **Schedule:** Boardroom Meeting (Intellect), Business Travel (Phys), Late Night Work (Ment), High-Stakes Negotiation (Soc).  
  * **Reaction:** She will initially ignore the condition as a "minor health issue." Once Gestation \> 40%, she attempts to maintain her schedule through sheer force of will, resulting in agonizing public waddling during high-pressure meetings.  
* **Unique Vulnerability: Cognitive Dissonance.** Effects that cause "Pregnancy Brain" (Fog, Nesting, or Infantilization) are 2x more effective. The contrast between her sharp mind and her new biological reality generates massive internal conflict and Biomass.

## **5\. Archetype: The Fitness Influencer (The "Gym Bunny")**

**Narrative Backdrop:** Her body is her business. With millions of followers watching her "fitness journey," she prides herself on her rock-hard abs and "flawless" workout routines. Her punishment is a total morphological takeover—her once-lean frame becoming a massive, heavy, and visibly labored vessel that no amount of "fit-tea" or cardio can fix.

* **Baseline Stats:**  
  * **Phys:** 95 (Incredible baseline fitness and endurance)  
  * **Ment:** 40 (Validation-dependent; obsessively checks her image)  
  * **Fin:** 60 (Sponsorship deals and ad revenue)  
  * **Soc:** 80 (Large digital following; zero physical privacy)  
* **Behavioral Pattern:**  
  * **Schedule:** Gym Session (Phys), Sponsored Photo Shoot (Soc), Juice Bar (Soc), Yoga Studio (Phys).  
  * **Reaction:** She will attempt to "work off" the weight with increasingly desperate and labored gym sessions. This causes massive Discomfort spikes as she tries to perform squats or yoga while terminally burdened.  
* **Unique Vulnerability: Biomechanical Burden.** Physical nodes (like Pelvic Floor Pressure or Respiratory Gasping) are 1.5x more effective because her AI *refuses* to stop her active lifestyle, leading to more frequent and humiliating physical "failures" in public.
