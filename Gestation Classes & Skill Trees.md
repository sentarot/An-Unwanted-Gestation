# **TECHNICAL GDD: Gestation Types & Skill Trees**

**Design Mandate 1: The "Flawless Vessel" Rule**

*Host visual fidelity must remain flawlessly attractive at all times. There is to be absolutely no gore, no grotesque anatomical mutations, and no physical degradation of the face or limbs. Horror elements must be driven strictly by impossible physical proportions (infinite abdominal and breast expansion), extreme gravity, hyper-exaggerated natural symptoms, and psychological subjugation. The Host should experience profound disgust at her own sheer volume and loss of control, even as she remains aesthetically pristine.*

**Design Mandate 2: The "Perpetual Motion" Rule**

*The Host must NEVER be permanently immobilized. Because the RPG loop relies on the Host executing her schedule and interacting with NPCs, she must always be able to move. She must remain on her (noticeably swollen) pretty little feet. Extreme gestation results in severely labored, agonizingly slow, and highly visible movement (the labored waddle).*

## **1\. The General Skill Tree (Universal Physiques & Stasis)**

The General\_Tree provides the foundational markers of the genre, focused on making the Host's body feel like a heavy, public prison before class-specific mutations are applied.

### **Branch A: Somatic Malady (Physical Strain & Proportions)**

* **Severe Heartburn (Cost: 15):** Payload pressure forces acid into the esophagus. Host winces and clutches her chest.  
* **Pregnancy Eructation (Burps) (Cost: 20):** Loud, involuntary eructations that shatter her sophisticated poise. High failure rate on "Formal" tasks.  
* **Gestational Flatulence (Farts) (Cost: 25):** Sluggish digestion and fetal pressure lead to frequent, audible gas. Generates massive passive Humiliation.  
* **Sciatic Nerve Compression (Cost: 35):** Payload settles on the nerve. Host's waddle is interrupted by sudden, sharp "zaps" that make her leg buckle.  
* **Round Ligament Strain (Cost: 45):** Agonizingly stretched supports. Host must move with extreme deliberation; any sudden turn causes her to gasp and clutch her sides.  
* **Pelvic Floor Pressure (Cost: 55):** The payload feels as if it is constantly about to "drop." Host adopts a wider, more labored stance and frequently presses her thighs together while walking.  
* **Center of Gravity Shift (Cost: 65):** Host adopts an extreme, agonizing Swayback posture, bracing her lower back with her hands even while standing still.  
* **Integumentary Tension (Cost: 80):** Skin becomes impossibly taut, shiny, and translucent. The belly appears "balloon-like." Host winces at any contact.  
* **Respiratory Gasping (Cost: 100):** Payload crushes the diaphragm. Host is perpetually breathless. Dialogue is delivered in short, panting gasps.

### **Branch B: Endocrine & Aesthetic Sabotage (Public Humiliation)**

* **Thermal Flush & Glisten (Cost: 15):** Constant, pristine sweat and a deep facial flush. Disables all Stealth tasks.  
* **Bladder Compression (Cost: 30):** Host often fidgets or presses a hand to her lower abdomen. Forces frequent restroom detours, interrupting long tasks.  
* **Wardrobe Failure (Cost: 50):** Buttons pop and zippers fail. Host is forced into "Ill-Fitting" clothing tier, generating massive passive Humiliation.  
* **Gestational Ptyalism (Hypersalivation) (Cost: 65):** Excessive saliva. Speech becomes slightly slurred and "wet." \-40% success on "Authority" tasks.  
* **Involuntary Exertion Vocalizations (Cost: 80):** Host emits deep, guttural grunts and rhythmic pants with every step. Sounds as if she is on the verge of imminent delivery.  
* **Public Abdominal Undulation (Cost: 95):** Payload shifts with such violence that it ripples the taut skin. Host frequently gasps and clutches her midsection as she is "worked" from the inside.  
* **Chronic Hyper-Lactation (Cost: 110):** Massive stains consistently ruin expensive maternity wear. Drains Financial\_Resources and Dignity.  
* **Hormonal Incontinence (Cost: 130):** Spontaneous weeping or flustered panic. Replaces "Serious" dialogue with random emotional triggers.  
* **Social Objectification (Cost: 150):** NPCs stop making eye contact and focus exclusively on her abdomen. They feel entitled to touch her without permission. Permanently deletes Authority.

### **Branch C: Temporal Distortion (Clinical Labor Delay)**

* **Oxytocin Antagonism (Cost: 20):** Mutes internal signals for labor. Host experiences painful "False Labor" that goes nowhere. Max Gestation 115%.  
* **Cervical Seal Reinforcement (Cost: 50):** Structural lock prevents dilation despite the crushing weight. Host's abdomen "drops" further into the pelvis. Max Gestation 135%.  
* **Uterine Quiescence (Cost: 100):** Smooth muscles are chemically paralyzed. Body cannot generate contractions. Belly remains in a state of rock-hard tension. Max Gestation 160%.
